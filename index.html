The primary mission of this site is to spotlight the Anambra Innovation Week, hosted by Solution Innovation District. With a goal to reach 20,000 innovators from Anambra, Nigeria, and beyond, this event empowers participants to connect, learn, and stand a chance to win over 100 million Naira ($60,000) in awards!

The 2024 Innovation Week is dedicated to driving progress in technology and digital transformation, especially in tackling socio-economic challenges across Africa. The centerpiece event, "Battle 4 Solutions," calls upon innovators, entrepreneurs, and tech enthusiasts to create practical solutions for pressing issues in diverse sectors.
